Fish Haven Town Events


A man dies after being ejected from car

February 29, 2019, BY Jane Doe

Authorities are investigating a single-vehicle crash that killed a 41-year-old Montpelier man on Saturday.

The crash occurred at approximately 3:40 a.m. on U.S. Highway 89 just north of the town of Fish Haven in Bear Lake County.

According to the Idaho State Police, Russell J. Johnson was driving northbound on U.S. Highway 89 when he drove his 1997 Toyota Corolla off the west side of the road. The vehicle then hit an embankment and rolled.

Police said Johnson was not wearing a seatbelt and was ejected from the vehicle. He died from his injuries at the scene of the crash.

The incident remains under investigation by the Idaho State Police.
